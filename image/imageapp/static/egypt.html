"EGYPT"

Egypt, a transcontinental country spanning the northeast corner of Africa and the Sinai Peninsula in Asia, is renowned for its ancient civilization, which flourished along the banks of the Nile River. It is home to some of the world's most iconic landmarks, including the Pyramids of Giza, the Great Sphinx, and the temples of Luxor. With a rich cultural heritage that has influenced Europe, Africa, and Asia, modern Egypt is a blend of ancient traditions and contemporary influences. Its economy is a developing mixed economy, with tourism, agriculture, and the Suez Canal being major contributors.
