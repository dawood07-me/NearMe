"THAILAND"

Thailand is a country in Southeast Asia known for its tropical beaches, opulent royal palaces, ancient ruins, and ornate temples displaying figures of Buddha. It is often called the "Land of Smiles" due to the friendly and welcoming nature of its people. The country has a rich cultural heritage, and is famous for its delicious and diverse cuisine, vibrant festivals like Songkran, and the traditional martial art of Muay Thai. As a major tourist destination, Thailand's economy relies heavily on tourism and exports, with a significant presence in the agricultural and manufacturing sectors.
