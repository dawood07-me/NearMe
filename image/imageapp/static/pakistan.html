"PAKISTAN"

Pakistan, officially the Islamic Republic of Pakistan, is a country in South Asia with a diverse landscape, from the soaring peaks of the Himalayas and Karakoram in the north to the fertile Indus River plain and the arid deserts in the south. It is the fifth-most populous country in the world, with a rich history rooted in ancient civilizations like the Indus Valley. Its culture is a vibrant blend of traditions, influenced by its diverse ethnic groups and its Islamic identity, which is central to the nation's founding. The economy is categorized as a developing one, with a focus on textiles, agriculture, and a rapidly growing service sector.
