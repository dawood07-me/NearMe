"UNITED ARAB EMIRATES"

The United Arab Emirates is a federation of seven emirates known for its rapid modernization and futuristic architecture. It's a global hub for business and tourism, with major cities like Dubai and Abu Dhabi featuring iconic landmarks such as the Burj Khalifa and the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque. The country has successfully diversified its economy from a reliance on oil to a focus on finance, trade, and tourism. The UAE is a melting pot of cultures, attracting a large expatriate population with its tax-free income and high-quality of life.
